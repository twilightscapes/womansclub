Meetings are held on the first Monday of every month: 5:30 pm Social - 6:00 pm Meeting at St. Peter's by the Sea Church in the Parish Hall, 1909 15th Street, Gulfport

Qualifications for Membership
Membership is open to women who are at least 18 years of age. Members should support the ideals of the club and should exhibit good moral character and intelligence.
